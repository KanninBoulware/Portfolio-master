My Philosophy of Teaching

My philosophy of education is that all kids are extraordinary in their own way. They must also have a welcoming educational environment where they can truly develop intellectually, emotionally, and socially as well as thrive because of Skinner's theory of Behaviorism ,which states that any and all behavior can be affected by their environment(McLeod,2018). It is my desire to make this kind of environment where my students can meet their full potential. I also agree with Dewey's "hands-on" approach because there a lot of children that can be helped in the classroom through a means more of hands on learning.
